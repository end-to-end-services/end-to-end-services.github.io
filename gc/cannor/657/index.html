CanNor Grants and Contributions

Not available end-to-end online

View service inventory entry Visit service website

CanNor's call for Expressions of Interest (EOI) for new single or multi-year projects is managed by a dedicated team of professional staff across the three territories, and works alongside its partners and stakeholders to foster northern economic development. The Agency is committed to delivering on the Government of Canada's priority to create a strong, diversified and sustainable economy that creates jobs, growth and long-term prosperity for the benefit of Northerners and all Canadians. (Community Development,Business Development, Canadian Northern Economic Development Agency.)
