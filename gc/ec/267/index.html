Environmental Funding – Habitat Stewardship Program

Not available end-to-end online

View service inventory entry Visit service website

The Habitat Stewardship Program (HSP) provides funding for projects submitted by Canadians that contribute directly to the recovery objectives and population goals of species at risk listed on Schedule 1 of the Species at Risk Act (SARA) and prevent others from becoming a conservation concern. (Species at Risk, Environment and Climate Change Canada.)
