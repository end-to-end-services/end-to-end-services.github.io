The small and medium-sized (SME) Projects stream of the Climate Action Incentive Fund (CAIF) is delivering up to $106 million to support eligible SME to undertake retrofit projects in sectors such as building, transportation, industry, waste, agriculture and more. (Clean Growth and Climate Change Mitigation, Environment and Climate Change Canada.)
