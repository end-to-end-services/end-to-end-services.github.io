Ecological Gifts Program

Available end-to-end online

View service inventory entry Visit service website

Canada’s Ecological Gifts Program provides a way for landowners in Canada with ecologically sensitive land to protect nature and leave a legacy for future generations. Made possible by the terms of the Income Tax Act of Canada and the Quebec Taxation Act, it offers significant tax benefits to landowners who donate land or a partial interest in land to a qualified recipient. Recipients ensure that the land’s biodiversity and environmental heritage are conserved in perpetuity. (Habitat Conservation and Protection, Environment and Climate Change Canada.)
