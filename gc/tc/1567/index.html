Defects and recalls of vehicles, tires and child car seats

Not available end-to-end online

View service inventory entry Visit service website

Transport Canada provides Canadians the opportunity to look up recalls, report a potential safety defect, find results of defect investigations, and get recall updates and alerts. These include safety recalls database, child car seat or vehicle recall status, safety advisories, defect reporting, investigation results. This complete online using our defect complaint form and through the 1-800 number available Mon-Friday. (Multi-Modal and Road Safety Oversight Program, Transport Canada.)
