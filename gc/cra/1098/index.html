Authorize a Representative

Available end-to-end online

View service inventory entry Visit service website

Due to the confidential nature of account information, a formal process is in place for taxpayers to authorize a representative to deal with income tax matters on their behalf. A request can be made at any time to both authorize or cancel an authorized representative. (Tax Services and Processing, Canada Revenue Agency.)
