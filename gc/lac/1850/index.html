International Standard Music Number – ISMN

Not available end-to-end online

View service inventory entry Visit service website Suggest a correction

Issues International Standard Music Numbers (ISMN) - unique numerical identifiers for notated music (scores and sheet music) whether published in print, online or in other media. ISMNs are required for the sale and distribution of notated music in Canada. (Acquisition and processing of published heritage, Library and Archives Canada.)
