Registry Services

Available end-to-end online

View service inventory entry Suggest a correction

This service works closely with tribunal chairs and members to ensure that matters before a tribunal can be heard and disposed of in a timely and efficient manner and within statutory obligations. Services include: processing tribunal documents; maintaining and safeguarding tribunal records; providing information to the public regarding tribunal procedures; assisting in the scheduling and proceedings of tribunal hearings; and, assisting in communicating tribunal decisions to the parties and the public. These services are provided by ATSSC employees such as registry and case file experts. (Registry Services, Administrative Tribunals Support Service of Canada.)
