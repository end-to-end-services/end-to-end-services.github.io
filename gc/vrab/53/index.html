Review and Appeal hearings

Not available end-to-end online

View service inventory entry Visit service website Suggest a correction

The independent avenue of appeal for disability benefits decisions made by Veterans Affairs Canada (VAC). Two levels of appeal are provided: Review, and if the applicant remains dissatisfied, Appeal. The service applies rules and dispenses impartial decisions for Veterans, Canadian Armed Forces and Royal Canadian Mounted Police members, and their families. (Review and Appeal, Veterans Review and Appeal Board.)
