Dispute Resolution for Grain Producers

Not available end-to-end online

View service inventory entry Visit service website

If a producer (or someone delivering grain on the producer’s behalf) disagrees with a licensed primary elevator’s assessment of the grain’s quality, they have the right to dispute the assessment. This is done by asking that a representative sample of the grain be sent to the Canadian Grain Commission. (Grain Quality, Canadian Grain Commission.)
